Vancouver was built upon c̓əsnaʔəm, an ancient village and burial site located on the north arm of the Fraser River. c̓əsnaʔəm is one of the most meaningful storehouses of the history and culture of the Musqueam people. Three c̓əsnaʔəm exhibitions will bring the rich history of the Musqueam Nation to the attention of Aboriginal and non-Aboriginal audiences. Each exhibition will highlight an aspect of the significance of c̓əsnaʔəm.
